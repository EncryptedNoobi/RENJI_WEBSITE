The Lore

Behold the famed Mythical Beasts, who originally hail from a different dimension – called the Demo-Verse.

These famed Beasts, The Oni, The Dragon, and the Nine Tailed Fox were all part of a Great War a millennia ago. Each having their own faction of supporters.

The war turned so bloody and violent that an entity even more ancient than the beats had to step in. The Phoenix, in all it’s might and beauty, sacrificed its freedom to trap itself along with the Mythical Beats in the Demi-Verse for what appeared to be All of eternity.

However, cracks are beginning to form on their dimensional cages and they will soon be unleashed looking for new hosts/ avatars to wield their power.
